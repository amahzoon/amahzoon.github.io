Alireza Mahzoon is a Ph.D. candidate in the institute of Computer Science at the University of Bremen. He received the B.Sc. degree in Electrical engineering from the K. N. Toosi University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2013, and M.Sc. degree in Digital Systems from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Tehran (UT), Tehran, Iran, in 2016. He is currently a member of the Group of Computer Architecture (AGRA) working with Professor Rolf Drechsler. His research interests are formal verification, debugging, arithmetic circuits, and high level synthsis.
